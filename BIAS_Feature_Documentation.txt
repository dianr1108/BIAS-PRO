--- FITUR ANALISIS CORE ---

1. PROFILE ANALYSIS (8 BEHAVIORAL LAYERS)
   Status: LIVE
   Route: /analysis

   Fitur Utama:
   - Analisis mendalam profil TikTok dengan 8 layer behavioral scoring (0-100)
   - SETIAP LAYER BISA DIKLIK untuk dapat detail analysis & actionable insights
   - AI-powered insights (GPT-4o-mini): Strength, Opportunity, Alert

   8 Behavioral Layers:
   1. Visual Layer - Thumbnail, color psychology, branding
   2. Audio Layer - Trending sounds, voice energy, music
   3. Energy Layer - Hook, pacing, video length
   4. Interaction Layer - Comment engagement, CTA, duet/stitch
   5. Linguistic Layer - Caption, hashtag, SEO
   6. Contextual Layer - Niche positioning, trend-jacking
   7. Environmental Layer - Posting times, location content
   8. Governance Layer - Bot detection (0-100%), brand safety

   Data Terekstrak:
   - Username, nickname, bio, avatar
   - Verified status (centang biru)
   - Followers, following, likes, video count
   - Link sosmed eksternal (IG, YouTube, X)
   - Enhanced scoring: follower/following ratio, multi-platform presence

   UI Components:
   - ProfileHeader dengan avatar & verified badge
   - Metric cards (followers, engagement, growth velocity)
   - Radar chart visualisasi 8 layer
   - Governance gauge untuk integrity score
   - AI insights panel (3-4 personalized recommendations)
   - Historical trend charts

   - Audio Quality (0-100) - Voice clarity, music sync, volume
   - Energy & Pacing (0-100) - Content rhythm, cut frequency
   - Viral Potential (0-100) - Prediksi chance viral

   AI-Powered Insights:
   - Kategori: Hook, Visual, Audio, Energy, Viral, Overall
   - Actionable suggestions: "Brighten +25%, saturation -10%"
   - Pattern recognition vs viral videos

   Actionable Editing Checklist:
   - Cut 0:00-0:02 (hook lemah)
   - Tambah text: "Rahasia 10K followers!" di 0:03
   - Brighten +25%, contrast +15%
   - Tambah zoom effect di 0:15 (punchline)
   - Sound: Ganti ke "Trending Sound #X"
   - End screen: "Part 2 coming!" + arrow up

   Features:
   - Drag & drop file upload (MP4, MOV, AVI)
   - Real-time analysis dengan progress
   - Download PDF report
   - Analysis history
